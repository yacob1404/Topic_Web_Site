What does your work at AFROTC entail?

That is a great question. Most often I spend my time in a class like most students
however we do participate in a lab section. In this lab section we practice everything
from room entry and clearing to busniess professionalism. It is a very large spectrum
of study. You can check out my detatchment here.


How often do you participate in AFROTC?

Cadet are required to attend one class and one lab section a week. However it is strongly
encouraged that every cadet attend IMT (initial military training), this is one hour a week.
Cadets who are AS200 (second year cadets) are strongly encouraged to attend both IMt and FTP
(Field Training Practice). FTP is held twice a week for an hour each time, here you will be
prepared for field training.


What exactly is Cyber Operations?

Cyber Operations is a program available at Dakota State University. This program details
offensive security measures in the pursuit of better security. The idea is you will know
how to protect your information if you know how to steal it. It is similar to protecting
your bank my trying to break into it. If you break into your own bank you should be able
to fix the security issues you used to break in.

What did you do at Computer Trouble Shooters of North Phoenix?

I spent most of my time setting up new Windows installations. This was a suprisingly time
consuming process. I also had to do basic hardware replacments, no soldering involved. Most
replacments were very basic from power supplies to R.A.M.


Are there any accomplishments at CTSNP you are proud of?

The whole experience was incredibly rewarding but one instance really sticks out. We had a
troubling HDD. This HDD would not let us delete a specific partition. Here is when I had to
learn about a program built into Windows. This program is accessed through the command prompt.
This program allowed me to delete the partition even though disk managment would not allow me to.
