What is AFROTC?

AFROTC stands for Air Force Reserves Officer Training Corps.
This is a program hosted by the United States Air Force to
train future Air Force officers while allowing cadets to obtain
a college degree.

What do we do?

In the AFROTC program cadets are trained and prepared to be leaders
in the Air Force. This program contains a class room portion and a lab.
In the class we are educated on various topics including history, war,
and leadership.
